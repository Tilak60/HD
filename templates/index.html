Heartbeat Detection System

How to Use

  • Upload and Analyze: Click "Choose File" to select an audio file (WAV or MP3) from your computer, then click "Upload and Analyze" to get a prediction and recommendation.
  • Audio Playback: After uploading, use the audio player controls to listen to your uploaded file.
  • Record Audio: Click "Start Recording" to record audio using your microphone. Click "Stop Recording" when finished. You can then play back and download your recording.
  • Waveform Visualization: Observe the waveform generated for both uploaded and recorded audio.
  • Analysis Results: View the predicted heart condition and a recommendation after analysis.
